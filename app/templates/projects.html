WhereWild

Senior capstone project combining iNaturalist observation data with GIS data to provide in-depth field guides and habitat insights for hundreds of thousands of species. Do in-depth analysis as to how species relate to the environment and each other, find species suitable for your region, and upload custom data with ease.

Summer 2024

PitchParse

Generates custom Japanese study decks by filtering vocab lists on frequency, minimal pairs, and learner status. Built to give pitch-accent learners structured practice based on guidance in the NHK Pitch Accent Dictionary. Ships with accent data compiled from various sources and a Yomitan dictionary to match.
